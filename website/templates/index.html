About Related Engine

Related Engine presents to you a variety of scientific research tool and scientific article finder to explore the extensive world of scholarly research articles. Whether you're a seasoned academic or a student embarking on a research journey, our tools simplify the process of finding related research papers, pull out article's citations or references, recommend articles for keywords, and more. Just be ready to handle the extent of data you could collect using our tools. We offer different ways to do your search, be it paper title, keywords, DOI, PubMed ID, or PubMed links. We'll help you discover interconnected research work in your field. Plus, with the added features of downloading your search results as a table with direct download and access links, references, and author's details, you can easily organize and reference your findings for future reference. We also offer a feature to view the results as a network graph for visual representations of the connections between search results.
